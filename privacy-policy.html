Privacy Policy for Smpl Journal

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

Overview

Smpl Journal ("the App") is a personal journaling application that helps users track their daily activities, expenses, and tasks.

Information Collection and Use

The App operates primarily offline and stores all user data locally on the device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information.

Data Storage

  • All journal entries, expenses, and tasks are stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers by default
  • If you choose to enable cloud sync features, data is stored in your personal Firebase account that you control

Permissions Used

Camera Permission (android.permission.CAMERA)

  • Purpose: Reserved for future features (QR code scanning for pairing devices)
  • Current Use: Not actively used in the current version
  • User Control: You can deny this permission and the app will function normally

Internet Permission

  • Purpose: Optional cloud synchronization via Firebase (if enabled by user)
  • Current Use: Only used when you explicitly enable sync features
  • User Control: App works fully offline; internet only used if you enable sync

Your Data Rights

  • Ownership: You own all data created in the app
  • Control: All data remains on your device unless you enable sync
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all local data
  • Export: You can export your data anytime via the app's export feature

Third-Party Services

If you choose to enable synchronization:

  • Firebase (Google) may store your encrypted data
  • Subject to Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted in the app and on this page.
